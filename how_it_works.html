How It Works

General FAQs

HOW MANY DAYS CAN I RENT?

You can rent a dress for 4, 7, or 14 days. We recommend a 7-day rental for most occasions, giving you time to enjoy the dress before returning it.

IS PROFESSIONAL CLEANING INCLUDED?

Yes! Professional cleaning is included in your rental fee. Simply return the dress in the condition you received it - no need to clean it yourself. We handle all the professional dry cleaning.

DO YOU OFFER TRY ONS?

Absolutely! We offer in-store try-on appointments by booking online. You can also order a backup size with your rental to ensure the perfect fit. Visit our 'Book a Try On' page to schedule your appointment.

Delivery

CAN I CHANGE MY SHIPPING ADDRESS

Yes, you can change your shipping address up to 24 hours before your rental starts.

WHAT ADDRESS DO I RETURN MY ITEM(S) TO?

You can return your item(s) to any Australia Post location. We provide a pre-paid return label with your rental, making it easy to send back. Just attach the label and drop it off at your nearest post office.

DO YOU DELIVER TO AUSTRALIA NATION-WIDE?

Currently, we only deliver within Brisbane, Australia. We are working on expanding our delivery options to include interstate shipping in the future.

Returns

HOW DO I RETURN MY DRESS?

Returning your dress is easy! Simply use the pre-paid return label included with your rental. Attach the label to the package and drop it off at your nearest Australia Post location. Make sure to return it by the end of your rental period to avoid late fees.

WHAT HAPPENS IF I RETURN MY DRESS LATE?

If you return your dress late, a late fee will be applied. The fee is $20 per day, up to a maximum of 5 days. After 5 days, the full retail price of the dress will be charged. To avoid late fees, please ensure you return your dress on time.

WHAT IF MY RENTAL PERIOD ENDS ON A SUNDAY OR A PUBLIC HOLIDAY?

If your rental period ends on a Sunday or public holiday, you can return your dress the next business day without incurring a late fee. We understand that postal services may not operate on these days, so we allow an extra day for returns. Just make sure to drop it off at the post office on the next business day.
