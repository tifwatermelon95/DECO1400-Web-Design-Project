About Us

Circularity was founded by Tiffany, a second-year Computer Science and Commerce student at the University of Queensland, with a prior degree in Bachelor of Pharmacy (Honours). After several years working as a clinical pharmacist, she realised her true passion lay at the intersection of technology, business, and creative problem-solving.

With a deep love for fashion, music (piano, violin, singing), interior design, and staying active through gym, pilates, swimming, hiking, and rock climbing — Tiffany is someone who thrives on expression and movement. Circularity was born out of a practical frustration: a packed calendar of weddings and events, each with its own dress code, and the growing guilt of buying new outfits she’d only wear once or twice.

Seeing that many women shared the same struggle, she set out to create something smarter. Circularity — a play on the term "circular economy" — offers stylish, sustainable dress rentals that let you look your best without the waste or wardrobe clutter.
